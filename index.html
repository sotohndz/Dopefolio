Projects Here are my projects that I'd like to showcase and give you a better understanding of me.

Flux

Flux is a dynamic web portal designed to streamline the process of lending cars and motorcycles. This innovative platform serves as a bridge between vehicle owners and borrowers, offering a user-friendly interface for seamless vehicle lending transactions. As a Full-Stack Developer, I led the development of both the front-end and back-end components. The portal features a robust search functionality to match borrowers with their desired vehicle type, make, and model, alongside real-time availability updates.

IT Link

IT Link is a comprehensive admin dashboard designed to streamline operations for an IT outsourcing agency. This dashboard serves as a centralized platform for managing client projects, tracking developer assignments, and monitoring project timelines and budgets. As a Full-Stack Web Developer, my role involved developing both the frontend user interface and the backend data management system. The dashboard is tailored to meet the unique needs of IT outsourcing, facilitating efficient workflow management and enhancing operational transparency.

Wanderlust Gateway

Wanderlust Gateway is an intuitive and user-friendly travel booking website designed to simplify the process of planning and booking various aspects of travel. The platform caters to globetrotters seeking a seamless experience in organizing their journeys, offering a one-stop solution for flights, hotels, and local experiences. As a lead developer, I was responsible for bringing the vision of an all-encompassing travel portal to life. My focus was on creating a platform that not only provided extensive functionality but also delivered a delightful user experience.
